## Use Cases

### For Journalists

**Preserving Evidence & Sources**

- Crawling during research to preserve potential sources
- Preserving cited pages for fact-checking and verification
- Creating permanent records of breaking news and investigations
- Archiving content that may be taken down or edited

**Example Workflows:**
- Real-time archiving during investigations
- Scheduled archiving of news sources and social media
- Building evidence archives with chain-of-custody tracking

### For Lawyers

**Legal Evidence Collection**

- Collecting and preserving digital evidence for cases
- Detecting changes in web content over time
- Tagging and organizing evidence for review
- Maintaining chain-of-custody for admissibility

**Features Lawyers Need:**
- Timestamped snapshots with cryptographic verification
- Audit logging for compliance and legal standards
- Secure access controls and permissions
- Export capabilities for court submissions

### For Researchers

**Academic & Data Research**

- Analyzing social media trends and online behavior
- Gathering training data for machine learning models
- Building web crawling pipelines for research projects
- Preserving primary sources for long-term studies

**Research Capabilities:**
- Bulk archiving and scheduled crawls
- API access for programmatic control
- Multiple export formats for analysis
- Integration with research workflows

### For Individuals

**Personal Digital Preservation**

- Saving bookmarks and reading lists
- Preserving portfolio content and creative work
- Legacy planning and digital memoirs
- Backing up personal social media content

**Individual Features:**
- Easy browser extension integration
- Simple one-click archiving
- Personal archive ownership and control
- Export and migration capabilities

### For Governments

**Public Records & Compliance**

- Snapshotting public service websites
- Meeting recordkeeping compliance requirements
- Preserving government communications and documents
- Maintaining historical records of policy changes

**Government Requirements:**
